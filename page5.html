Reduce our footprint

Lets look at...

Some other steps we can all take daily to reduce ocean pollution such as:

  • Use public transport
  • Cycle if you can
  • Car pool
  • Drive an electric vehicle or hybrid
  • Save energy in your home
  • Minimize food waste
  • Reduce water waste
  • Plant more trees

If we can try to do 2 or 3 of these steps each we will be on our way to a cleaner ocean. We can hope for a better future. There are sub goals of the SDG 14 life below water they include:

  • By 2025 reduce significantly marine pollution
  • Protect marine and ecosystems to prevent permanent damage
  • Minimize ocean acidification
  • Regulate fishing and end over fishing
  • Conserve 10% at least of coastal and marine areas
  • Eliminate unregulated fishing sustainable fishing
  • Help develop small island economies by allowing them to benefit from cleaner waters
  • Increase marine research
